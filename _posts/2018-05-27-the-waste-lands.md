This is the third instalment in Stephen King's The Dark Tower series which I am gradually working through and was probably my favourite so far.
The series has picked up significantly from the first instalment which took me more than one attempt to read in full.

The mystery surrounding this series (and my desire to avoid spoilers) is growing significantly, to the extent that I am having to make a conscious effort not to buy the fourth book straight away.
As, I find, with all Stephen King books you can see his horror chops coming through but it goes without saying that the man is a natural story teller.

All in all a great series so far -- just need to let myself read some different things before I start the next one.
