## Ingredients (2 portions)

- 3 Star Anise
- Cinnamon Stick
- Kaffir Lime Leaves (3 or 4)
- Soy Sauce (a glug)
- Spring Onions 3
- 5 Slices of fresh ginger
- 2 Veggie stock cubes
- 2 teaspoons of miso paste
- Fresh coriander
- A pack of nice mushrooms (I like enoki and shiitake)
- A head of brocolli
- Tofu fried (like in the [stir fry](./stir_fry) recipe)
- Rice noodles
- Optionals: thai basil/mint/chopped chilli

## Recipe

- Add the stock cubes, star anise, cinnamon stick, lime leaves, soy sauce, chopped spring onions (the white bit), ginger, the chopped stems of the coriander, and miso paste to about 1 litre of water.
- Boil this for like 30-45 minutes (this is super-powering the stock).
- Sieve the solid from the stock and put the stock back on the heat.
- Add the shiitake mushrooms and cook for 10 minutes.
- Prepare the noodles separately based on what the pack says.
- After the mushrooms have boiled for ten minutes, add the broccoli to cook for 10 minutes.
- With like 3 minutes left on the broccoli time add the tofu and the enoki mushrooms.
- Put the noodles in a big bowl and with a ladle pour the mushroom/broccoli/tofu loveliness over it.
- Serve topped with some chopped coriander, chopped whites of the spring onion, and some of the optionals if you want.
